Our Mission

We support and inspire the student entrepreneurs, pioneers, innovators, and creators of Northwestern. We curate inspirational events, host our annual venture challenge competition, and bring networking and job opportunities to campus. We want to give all students of all disciplines the opportunity to experience entrepreneurship during their time at Northwestern and respond in epic ways. We want to build a community at Northwestern that believes in better – better design, better products, better services, and better companies. We’ll do this one event, one course, one network, one idea, and one student entrepreneur at a time.
